Rising Sun Tours Japan
Rising Sun Tours is my passion project in tourism — currently focused on Japan, with global ambitions for the future. The goal is to help travelers discover Japan's beauty authentically: its hidden gems, cultural depth, seasonal wonders, and the warmth of its people that you can't find in a guidebook.

From planning itineraries for first-time visitors to sharing travel insights for Japan enthusiasts — this is where my love for travel meets my drive to help others experience it too.
